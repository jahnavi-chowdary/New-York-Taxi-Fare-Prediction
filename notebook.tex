https://www.mapdevelopers.com/geocode\_bounding\_box.php

The boundaries of New York are:\\
North Latitude: 40.917577\\
South Latitude: 40.477399\\
East Longitude: -73.700272\\
West Longitude: -74.259090

Used the above to build a boundary by for the allowed Drop\_off and
Pick\_up Longitude, Latittudes.

analysing the possible reasons for the bad behaviour of the model, I

samples had coordinates outside the above used boundary.

boundaries on the Lattitude and Longitude.

North Latitude: 41.709555\\
South Latitude: 40.573143\\
East Longitude: -72.986532\\
West Longitude: -74.263242
